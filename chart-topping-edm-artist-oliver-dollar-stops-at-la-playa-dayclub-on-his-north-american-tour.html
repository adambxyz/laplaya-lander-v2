Oliver Dollar Stops At La Play Dayclub

Report to the dance floor. On Saturday, June 18, 2022 La Playa Dayclub opens its heart and its infamous “Tree House DJ Booth” to Oliver Dollar, an internationally known, chart-topping artist from Berlin. Often described as a passionate beat maker and booty shaker, this event will be for all dancers and music lovers in South Florida.


“I am excited to return to the states on this tour and to be playing in Fort Lauderdale,” says Oliver Dollar. “I have been hearing about their rapidly growing music scene and am honored to support a new venue that supports house music.”


La Playa Dayclub is quickly gaining momentum and followers as they attract a new music-loving community to Fort Lauderdale Beach through creative marketing videos, community engagement, and peer-to-peer word of mouth.


“It is about quality music. That is what we focus on and what we can bring to Fort Lauderdale,” says talent buyer and resident DJ Jonathan Cowan. “What we want to bring to our guests at La Playa Dayclub is insanely great music, every single weekend, featuring some of the most talented DJs and producers in the world.”


Doors will open at 12 pm and guests can enjoy not only great music but unique food and beverage options and for those wanting an elevated luxury experience through our VIP table service packages.


“In the years I have lived in South Florida, I have witnessed the music scene here grow and evolve, and what is happening now is super exciting,” says La Playa Dayclub resident DJ, Mike Locke, the #1 DJ in Fort Lauderdale, according to Fort Lauderdale Magazine. I would have never thought we would have a house music club right on Fort Lauderdale Beach, and now we have La Playa Dayclub showcasing some of our finest local talent alongside world-renowned DJ’s at our parties every weekend.”

Tickets are on sale now and can be purchased at - https://link.laplayaday.club/oliver-dollar-june-18.


About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

 

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit https://laplayadayclub.com/.
