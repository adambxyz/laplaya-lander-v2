Leading South Florida DJs Form Afro-Latin Super Group called The Puto Boyz and Instantly Draw Crowds

Get ready to dance under the stars! This Saturday, July 23, 2022, a brand new, South Florida, house music supergroup called The Puto Boyz headlines the hottest day club in South Florida, La Playa Dayclub.

Get ready to dance under the stars!  This Saturday, July 23, 2022, a brand new, South Florida, house music supergroup called The Puto Boyz headlines the hottest day club in South Florida, La Playa Dayclub.


The Puto Boyz is a group consisting of well-known music producers and DJs David Pomareda & Vitto Santillan.  Both of these artists were born in Lima, Peru, and moved to South Florida. Meeting years later in the Miami house music scene, they quickly became friends and formed this new group. With days in the making, this highly sought-after duo has booked shows throughout South Florida, stopping at La Playa Dayclub for a very special headlining set that will truly encapsulate their sound and vibe.


“I had the idea of creating a duo with Afro-Latin Vibes, says David Pomareda, founder and one of the members of The Puto Boyz. “I was on a trip to Machu Picchu and was instantly inspired as the sound is very similar to Afro-Peruvian music which I also enjoy very much.”


This show is comprised of a full day of events right on the gorgeous South Florida coastline on the South Point of Fort Lauderdale Beach. With brunch kicking off the day from 11am - 3pm and afternoon and evening programming beginning at 3pm, guests are encouraged to come to dine, eat and dance on this second-floor open-air venue just steps from the beach. Other artists featured this day are Colombian female powerhouse Hanna Leon, a legend of house music in Palm Beach, J Martinez, and up-and-coming Miami producer Alfredo Yanez.


“We have worked with Techneekz (an alias of David Pomareda) for years. He owns a successful label, has played shows across the country, and brings a great vibe and energy to the stage,” says Jason Shaw, CEO of Round Table Marketing Group, a premier event marketing and production company in South Florida.  “The Afro-Latin sound and style of The Puto Boyz is great and the perfect vibe for a beach party.” 


Admission to this event is FREE for anyone 21 and over who is on the guest list. South Florida residents and music fans can sign up for our guest list HERE.

About La Playa Dayclub
