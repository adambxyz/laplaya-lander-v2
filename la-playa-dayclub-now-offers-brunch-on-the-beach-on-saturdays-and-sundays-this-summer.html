La Playa Dayclub Now Offers Brunch On The Beach Saturdays & Sundays This Summer.

Join us at La Playa Dayclub on Saturdays and Sundays from 11 AM - 3 PM for brunch on the beach! Enjoy delicious selections from our brand new brunch menu and tunes from our talented resident DJs.

Join us at La Playa Dayclub on Saturdays and Sundays from 11 AM - 3 PM for brunch on the beach! Enjoy delicious selections from our brand new brunch menu and tunes from our talented resident DJs.


Perfect for adult groups (21+) ranging in size between 4-12, this open-air, second-floor terrace is the perfect place to enjoy the weekend and catch the “La Playa Vibe.” Offering a wide assortment of food, drinks, and VIP bottle options, it is quickly becoming the best place on the beach to celebrate a special occasion or to grab a bite with your friends and loved ones.


This exclusive brunch menu, designed by Executive Chef Eddie Guerra, will include favorites from our afternoon and evening menu along with some delicious new offerings. “It is important for us to offer our guests and beach goers a little something for everyone,” says Chef Guerra, “Our property [La Playa Dayclub & Bo’s Beach] is a really great place to gather with your family and friends. Having food that everyone can enjoy is important to us.”


With a wide variety on the menu, our guests can choose from a classic Steak and Eggs and Designable Skillets or new items like a Breakfast Pizza which comes right out of the wood-fired oven, our Wildberry Waffles, (perfect for those with a sweet tooth), and the Hog & Hen which is the perfect hangover cure! The Hog & Hen includes bacon, free-range eggs, bourbon maple syrup, and aged Wisconsin cheddar, all between a Belgian waffle bun.


If you love the taste of seafood while watching the waves roll onto the shore, try our Fresh Oysters, Shrimp Ceviche, Ahi Tuna Stacks, Smoked Fish Dip, Calamari, Stone Crab Chowder, Blackened Mahi-Mahi Sandwiches, Fish Tacos, Fish & Chips, Coconut Shrimp, our local Catch of the Day or even our famous Lobster Rolls.


“Our place is simply awesome for brunch,” says Brando Garcia, manager of La Playa Dayclub. “We have great food, talented DJs, the best bartending staff on the beach plus our incredible views. Whether you are looking for a quick bite or a boozy brunch that lasts all day, we have you covered!”


Seating for brunch is reservation only and can be made in advance by clicking this link or by sending an email to viplaplayadayclub(at)gmail.com. Guests must be 21 years of age or older.


“It is all about creating a fun environment,” says Brian Gallant also known as DJ Mas Inc and is one of La Playa’s resident DJs. ”People come to La Playa Dayclub to enjoy the day and our beautiful beaches. They drink, dance, eat and just have a good time.”

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.


Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit https://laplayadayclub.com/.
