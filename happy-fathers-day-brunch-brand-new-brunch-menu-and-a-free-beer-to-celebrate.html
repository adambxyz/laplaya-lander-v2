Happy Father's Day Brunch | Brand New Brunch Menu (and a free beer) To Celebrate!

Join us at La Playa Dayclub on Sunday, June 19 from 11am - 3pm as we celebrate the ones we love most with great food, delicious drinks, stunning views, and even FREE BEER!

Join us at La Playa Dayclub on Sunday, June 19 from 11am - 3pm as we celebrate the ones we love most with great food, delicious drinks, stunning views, and even FREE BEER!


This exclusive brunch menu, designed by Executive Chef Eddie Guerra, will include favorites from our afternoon and evening menu along with some delicious new offerings. “It is important for us to offer our guests and beach goers a little something for everyone,” says Chef Guerra, “Our property [La Playa Dayclub & Bo’s Beach] is a really great place to gather with your family and friends. Having food that everyone can enjoy is important to us."


Designed for the Dad who Brunches:

With brunch now offered at La Playa Dayclub, on the 2nd-floor rooftop of Bo’s Beach, our Fathers will not only be treated to the best view of the beach in Fort Lauderdale but also great food. La Playa Dayclub has kept items like the classic Steak and Eggs and Designable Skillets and is also introducing new items like a Breakfast Pizza which comes right out of the wood-fired oven, Wildberry Waffles, (perfect for the dad with the sweet tooth), and the Hog & Hen which is good for the Dad who partied too long Saturday night at La Playa Dayclub and needs that hangover cure! The Hog & Hen includes bacon, free-range eggs, bourbon maple syrup, and aged Wisconsin cheddar, all between a Belgian waffle bun.


Designed for the Dad who loves Seafood:

If your Dad is a fan of Seafood, we more than have you covered. During brunch, La Playa Dayclub offers a wide variety of seafood options including Oysters, Shrimp Ceviche, Ahi Tuna Stacks, Smoked Fish Dip, Calamari, Stone Crab Chowder and Manhattan Clam Chowder, Blackened Mahi-Mahi Sandwiches, Fish Tacos, Fish & Chips, Coconut Shrimp, our local Catch of the Day and of course our famous Lobster Rolls.


Designed for Dad who loves Meat

With a variety of options for a meat lover, our guests and their Dad will definitely find something they will all enjoy. From our Delicious Burger options to a Churrasco Steak, a true South Florida favorite, or even a Beach Supreme Pizza, our options are endless for a very hungry Dad.


Designed for a really great Dad.

Let La Playa Dayclub help you celebrate your Dad by buying him the first beer and toasting him on this special day.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.


Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit https://laplayadayclub.com/.
