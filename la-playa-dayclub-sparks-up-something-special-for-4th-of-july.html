La Playa Dayclub Sparks up Something Special for 4th of July

La Playa Dayclub sparks up something special for 4th of July weekend with a popular Friday Island Vibes Reggae Happy Hour, new food menus, and the largest fireworks viewing party in Fort Lauderdale on Monday, the 4th of July.

La Playa Dayclub sparks up something special for 4th of July weekend with a popular Friday Island Vibes Reggae Happy Hour, new food menus, and the largest fireworks viewing party in Fort Lauderdale on Monday, the 4th of July.


“We are ready for a big weekend and our first time celebrating Fort Lauderdale with our beach community,” says Brando Garcia. “We have a great lineup of djs and the best views and drinks on the beach!”


La Playa Dayclub kicks off festivities on Friday, July 1st with an “Island Vibes” Reggae Happy Hour, featuring South Florida’s very own DJ Impulse Disco and the award-winning number 1 reggae radio host in the US, Tanto Irie! Featuring our brand new extended Happy Hour starting at 5 PM our guests can enjoy $5 draft beers, $7 Titos cocktails, and $9 appetizers all while they sit back, relax and enjoy the island vibes.


On Saturday July 2nd, and Sunday, July 3rd we invite our guests to awaken their sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La playa Dayclub. Dance to FTLs hottest house music DJs while enjoying gorgeous beach views and the cool ocean breeze in your hair. Brunch and lunch are offered from 11 AM -3 PM and reservations are required. Table and bottle service is also available from 3 PM -10 PM can be reserved online by clicking here.


On Monday, July 4th, join us for an extra special friends and family day at La Playa Dayclub as we host a viewing party that runs from 2 PM -10 PM for the best fireworks shows in South Florida. With fireworks happening right outside, and house music all day long, our guests can eat, drink, dance, and celebrate their independence with the incredible La Playa Community. Space is limited, so we ask that guests RSVP for this special event HERE.


“4th of July on the beach is always a good time,” says resident DJ Mas Inc. “And now with La Playa Dayclub adults can enjoy the day in a great space with a drink in hand dancing with their friends.”
