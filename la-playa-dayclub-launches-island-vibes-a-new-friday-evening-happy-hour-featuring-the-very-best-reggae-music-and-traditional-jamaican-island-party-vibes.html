Announcements

La Playa Dayclub launches “Island Vibes”, a new Friday evening Happy Hour featuring the very best Reggae.

This new Friday night kicks off weekly at 4 PM with happy hour beginning at 5 PM. Hosted by a legend of reggae music, Tanto Irie, and supported by Reggae Runnins, Hot 105 and a variety of well-known reggae artists, Island Vibes will be the place to be every Friday!

This new Friday night kicks off weekly at 4 PM with happy hour beginning at 5 PM. Hosted by a legend of reggae music, Tanto Irie, and supported by Reggae Runnins, Hot 105 and a variety of well-known reggae artists, Island Vibes will be the place to be every Friday!


Hosted by Tanto Irie, a Lifetime Achievement Award winner for the International Reggae & World Music Awards and host of the number one reggae show in South Florida, Friday nights at La Playa Dayclub promises to be full of great music and authentic Irie vibes. “Reggae music is in my soul,” says Tanto. “I grew up in Kingston, Jamaica listening to live reggae music and devoted my life to spreading the music, its message, and many of the talented artists. From my weekly radio show “Reggae Runnins” on Hot 105 (21+ years running) to organizing some of South Florida’s largest Caribbean festivals, if there is reggae music I'm there! And if there is a cool spot with a great team, like La Playa Dayclub, who really wants to create an authentic reggae experience, then I am proud to help however I can.”


While guests sit back and unwind on our open-air rooftop, complete with gorgeous beachfront views, they are taken through a musical journey by Tanto’s favorite South Florida Reggae DJs. “It’s important for us not only as entertainment producers but also as local community members, to offer unique experiences on the beach for both our locals and tourists,” says CEO of Round Table Marketing Group Jason Shaw. “Our Fridays are designed for those travelers and beach goers who have hit the beach all day and now want to cool off with some great food and delicious drinks while enjoying a true South Florida / Caribbean vibe.”


For those who adventure out in the early evening, we have our Reggae Happy Hour which goes 5 PM - 8 PM and features $5 draft beers, $7 Tito's Cocktails, and $9 appetizers from our kitchen.


Oh, and don’t be surprised to see some of today’s hottest reggae artists as La Playa Dayclub quickly establishes itself as the hotspot for the South Florida Reggae community.

About La Playa Dayclub

Awaken your sense of discovery as you step into the intimate jungle oasis of La Playa Dayclub, Fort Lauderdale Beach’s first true luxury day club experience. Nestled on the 2nd floor of Fort Lauderdale’s most beloved beachfront restaurant, Bo's Beach, this hidden sanctuary calls out to travelers, adventurers, and wanderers alike.

Dance alongside the setting sun, immersed in lush foliage and the rhythmic vibrations of the world’s best international House Music DJs. With our breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale Beach, this paradise found is a haven for the daring voyager inside us all. To learn more about La Playa Dayclub, please visit https://laplayadayclub.com/.
